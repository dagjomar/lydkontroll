# Decision Log

Record choices that future work should not have to rediscover. Use the next
sequential ID and link the relevant task or plan.

## Template

### ADR-NNN: Short decision title

- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded
- **Task:** TASK-NNN
- **Context:** What forces the choice?
- **Decision:** What are we doing?
- **Consequences:** What becomes easier, harder, or constrained?
- **Alternatives:** What was considered and why was it rejected?
